Catrine Primary Poppy Project

The Poppy project was to commemorate the 100th anniversary of WW1, a project which the pupils in primary 7 were covering.

We took line drawings of poppies the children had created and transformed them into lino print blocks using our laser cutter. The children then used the lino blocks to print their poppy images onto fabric, which was used to make a banner for their remembrance service.

The children created some amazing drawings and their final piece was fantastic.
