The pack contains:

  • 47 detailed lesson plans
  • Summary of Covered Outcomes
  • Large Resource Box
  • Access to online resources via our website

Each Lesson is comprised of the following sections:

  • What's Happening? - A summary of what pupils will do during the lesson.
  • Experiences and Outcomes and Related Benchmarks.
  • Pupils will Learn - Helpful for setting Success Criteria.
  • Resources Required - The majority of these come with the pack. Other items should already be in most schools.
  • Introduction - Ideas on how to introduce the lessons.
  • Development - Suggestions for how to move the lessons along.
  • Plenary - A guide to summarising the learning.
  • Assessment - Identifies opportunities to assess children.
  • Differentiation - Basic ideas to support and challenge pupils.
  • Useful Questions and Answers- Suggested questions to ask pupils complete with answers.
  • Why Teach This - Identifies links to real life.

Pupil Support materials are clear and easy to follow.
